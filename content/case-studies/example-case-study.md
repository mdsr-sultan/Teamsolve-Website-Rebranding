# TeamSolve Announces Partnership with Mega Western Sales, Inc.

TeamSolve is proud to announce a strategic partnership with Mega Western Sales, Inc., a leading provider of water infrastructure solutions.

## The Challenge

Mega Western Sales needed to ensure their contractors had instant access to accurate installation information to minimize downtime and ensure warranty compliance.

## The Solution

By implementing Knowledge Twin™, TeamSolve provided Mega Western Sales with:

- Instant access to installation procedures
- Real-time guidance for field teams
- Automated quality checks
- Enhanced field support capabilities

## The Results

"With the Knowledge Twin, our contractors can instantly access the right information at the right time, enhancing installation accuracy. This minimizes downtime, ensures warranty compliance, and transforms field support."

— Tom Duddy, Mega Western Sales Principal

## Impact

- Reduced installation errors by 40%
- Improved warranty compliance
- Faster field support response times
- Enhanced contractor satisfaction
